About This Project

Smart Drainage Management System uses IoT-based sensors and a machine learning model (Random Forest) to detect drainage blockages in real time. Alerts are sent to municipal authorities to ensure quick action.

Technologies: Python, Flask, HTML/CSS, SQLite, Chart.js

Team: AquaFlow Analytics
